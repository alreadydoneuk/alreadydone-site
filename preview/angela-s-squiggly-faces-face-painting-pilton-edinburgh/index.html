About Angela

A Friendly Face Behind Every Smile

Angela is a skilled and passionate face painter based in Pilton, Edinburgh, serving families and event organisers across Edinburgh, Inverkeithing, Fife, and the wider Lothians each and every weekend.

With years of experience painting children and adults alike, Angela has built a reputation for her wide range of designs, her gentle approach with shy children, and her ability to keep things running smoothly even at the busiest parties.

What truly sets Angela apart is her care from the very first enquiry to the last painted face. Fast, detailed communication, early arrival to set up, and a calm, creative energy that children and parents both love.
