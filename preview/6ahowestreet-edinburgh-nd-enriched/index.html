About Us

A passion for beautiful things, rooted in Edinburgh

We are a family-run antique shop based on Howe Street in Edinburgh's New Town, built on a genuine shared love of vintage objects, antique furniture, and original paintings carefully brought together over many years.

What sets us apart is the personal touch: every piece in the shop has been hand-picked with care, so you will never find a cluttered, impersonal selection here. We take pride in knowing the story behind what we sell.

Based in the heart of Edinburgh and open at weekends, we offer a free delivery service for larger items within the city, making it simple to bring something special home without the hassle.
