About Us

Skilled Tradespeople You Can Rely On

A-Class Glass and Joinery is a professional joinery and glazing company proudly based in Newhaven, Edinburgh — with years of hands-on experience serving homeowners and businesses across the city and surrounding areas.

What sets us apart is our commitment to doing the job properly: we use quality materials, offer honest pricing, and never cut corners on the finish — because we know that good craftsmanship speaks for itself.

We're locals ourselves, and we understand what Edinburgh properties demand. From the tenement flats of Leith to the family homes of Trinity and Inverleith, we're your neighbourhood joiners who genuinely care about the outcome.

What We Do

Our Services

We cover a broad range of joinery and glazing work for residential and commercial customers across Edinburgh. No job is too small, and every project gets the same high standard of care.

🪟

Window Glazing & Repair

Whether you need a single pane replaced, a full double-glazing unit fitted, or draught-proofing carried out, we handle all glazing work efficiently and neatly. We can also repair sash and case windows common across Edinburgh's older properties.

🚪

Door Fitting & Repair

From internal doors and external hardwood doors to fire doors and composite front doors, we supply, fit, and repair all types of doors. We ensure everything hangs true, closes securely, and keeps your property safe and weather-tight.

🪵

Bespoke Joinery & Carpentry

Need something made to measure? We craft bespoke staircases, skirting boards, architraves, shelving units, and fitted storage to suit your space perfectly. All work is finished to a high standard, ready to paint or stain as required.

🛠️

General Property Maintenance

From draughty loft hatches to damaged floorboards and loose bannisters, our experienced team handles a wide range of home maintenance tasks. If it's made of wood or glass, there's a good chance we can fix it for you quickly and affordably.
