About Us

Local Expertise You Can Trust

Paul D Shapero is a skilled and experienced painter and decorator based in Pilton, Edinburgh, with years of hands-on experience working across homes and properties throughout the city and surrounding areas.

Serving Edinburgh's north side and beyond, Paul covers everything from Pilton and Granton to Leith, Stockbridge, and further afield. Every job, large or small, receives the same attention to detail and personal service.

What sets Paul apart is a genuine commitment to doing the job properly: thorough preparation, quality materials, and a tidy finish that lasts. With weekend availability, getting quality decorating work done has never been more convenient.
