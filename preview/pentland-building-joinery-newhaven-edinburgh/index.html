About Us

Craftsmanship at the heart of Edinburgh

Pentland Building & Joinery is a family-run business based in Newhaven, Edinburgh, with years of hands-on experience delivering high-quality joinery and building services across the city and surrounding areas.

What sets us apart is our commitment to doing the job properly — no shortcuts, no substandard materials, and no vague timescales. Every project we take on gets the same care and attention we'd bring to our own homes.

We're proud to be part of the Newhaven community, and a significant share of our work comes through local recommendations — which tells you everything you need to know about how we operate.

Our Services

Doors, Frames & Staircases

Supply and fit of internal and external doors, new door frames, and bespoke staircase work — crafted to fit your home precisely and finished to a high standard.

🪑

Fitted Furniture & Storage

Made-to-measure wardrobes, shelving units, alcove cupboards, and home office solutions — designed around your space and built to last for years.

Windows & Sash Repairs
