About us

We train as one

CrossFit Edinburgh is the capital's ultimate CrossFit facility, founded by Pete with a single mission: to provide elite standard coaching to every ability, in an environment built to inspire. We believe in a higher standard, and that belief runs through everything we do, from the quality of coaching to the condition of the gym floor.

What sets us apart is our commitment to functional fitness done properly. Under the guidance of our experienced coaches, you will learn the power of the CrossFit methodology in a facility that is genuinely good, clean, swish and impressive. Whether you are brand new to CrossFit or a seasoned athlete, you will be coached and challenged with the same care and attention.

Based in Bonnington, Edinburgh, we are proud to be part of this community, welcoming members from across the city and beyond, including weekend training sessions for those with busy weekday schedules.

"Good, clean, swish and impressive" is not just a description. It is the standard we hold ourselves to, every single session.

What we offer

Memberships and programmes

🏋️

CrossFit Classes

Coach-led group sessions built around the CrossFit methodology. Every class is programmed to develop functional strength, conditioning and movement quality, scaled to suit all ability levels.

📋

Memberships

Flexible membership options designed to fit your life, including weekend access for those who need it most. All memberships include access to our full facility and unlimited coached classes.

🎯

Foundations Course

New to CrossFit? Our Foundations programme gives you the skills, confidence and technique to move safely and effectively before joining regular group classes. The perfect starting point.

🤝

Community & Competition

CrossFit Edinburgh trains as one. From friendly in-house challenges to local competition prep, we support members who want to go further, with coaching that meets you at your level.
