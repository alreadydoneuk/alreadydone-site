About Us

Edinburgh SEO, Done Right

Caliber Interactive Ltd is a specialist SEO consultancy based at Bonnington Bond in Edinburgh, working with businesses across Scotland and the UK to improve their search visibility and drive real, sustainable growth.

Unlike generic digital agencies, we focus exclusively on search engine optimisation. That means every strategy we build is grounded in technical expertise, up-to-date ranking knowledge, and a genuine understanding of what search engines reward.
