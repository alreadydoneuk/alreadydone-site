A Bit About Us

Part of the Canon Mills Community

We've been looking after Edinburgh's garments from our spot on Huntly Street for many years. What started as a straightforward local laundrette has grown into a full dry cleaning and laundry service that families and professionals across the New Town, Canonmills, and Stockbridge areas rely on week in, week out.

We're not a faceless chain. We're your local cleaners — the ones who know your name, remember your preferences, and take genuine pride in the work we do. Every item that comes through our door is handled with care, because we know how much your clothes matter to you.

Conveniently located at 7–8 Huntly Street, Edinburgh EH3 5HB, we're easy to reach whether you're walking through from Canonmills or popping in on your way through the New Town.
