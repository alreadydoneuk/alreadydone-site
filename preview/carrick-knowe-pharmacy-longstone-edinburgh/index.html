About Us

Your Local Independent Pharmacy

Carrick Knowe Pharmacy has been a cornerstone of the Longstone and Corstorphine community for years, offering the kind of personal, attentive service that only an independent pharmacy can provide — where staff know your name, not just your prescription number.

Unlike large chain chemists, we take the time to listen. Whether you have a question about a new medication, need advice on a minor ailment, or want to explore our health services, you'll always speak to a qualified pharmacist who genuinely cares about your wellbeing.

Situated on Saughton Road North, we serve the families and residents of Longstone, Corstorphine, Saughton, and the wider Edinburgh EH12 area — proud to be part of this community and committed to keeping it healthy.

What We Offer

Our Services

From dispensing your repeat prescription to same-day consultations, Carrick Knowe Pharmacy provides a full range of healthcare services for the local community.

💊

Prescription Dispensing

Fast, accurate dispensing of NHS and private prescriptions. We handle repeat prescriptions and can liaise directly with your GP surgery to save you time.

🩺

Pharmacy Consultations

No appointment needed. Our qualified pharmacists offer free, confidential advice on minor illnesses, medication queries, and health concerns — just walk in.

💉

Vaccinations & Travel Health

From seasonal flu jabs to travel vaccinations, we provide convenient, clinically administered immunisations to keep you protected all year round.

🛍️

Health & Wellbeing Products

A thoughtfully stocked range of vitamins, skincare, baby products, and OTC medicines — chosen to meet the everyday needs of Edinburgh families.
