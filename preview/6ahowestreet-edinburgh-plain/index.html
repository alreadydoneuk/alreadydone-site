About Us

A passion for antiques, rooted in Edinburgh

6aHoweStreet is a carefully curated antique shop situated in the heart of Edinburgh's New Town, offering a thoughtfully assembled collection of period furniture, decorative objects, ceramics, and collectibles from across the centuries.

What sets us apart is the personal attention given to every piece: each item is hand-selected for its quality, provenance, and character, so you are never browsing a generic stock room but a living collection with real stories behind it.

Proudly trading from Howe Street in Edinburgh's EH3, we are open at weekends and warmly welcome both seasoned collectors and curious newcomers who simply want to discover something special close to home.

What We Offer

Antiques and services for every collector

🪑

Period Furniture

From Georgian writing desks to Victorian sideboards, our furniture collection brings enduring craftsmanship into contemporary Edinburgh homes. Each piece is assessed for condition and authenticity before it reaches our floor.

🏺

Ceramics and Decorative Objects

Explore a rotating selection of fine ceramics, glassware, silver, and decorative arts. Whether you are seeking a single striking object or building a collection, you will find pieces to suit a range of tastes and budgets.

🔍

Sourcing and Finds

Looking for something specific? We are happy to help source particular styles, periods, or types of antique on your behalf, drawing on our network and experience to track down items that suit your brief.

💬

Friendly Expert Advice

Not sure what you are looking at or what something might be worth? We offer honest, knowledgeable guidance with no pressure. Whether you are buying, browsing, or simply curious, we welcome every question.
