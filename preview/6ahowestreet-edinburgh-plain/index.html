About Us

Character, craft, and a passion for the past

6aHoweStreet is an independent antique shop located at 6A Howe Street in the heart of Edinburgh's New Town, offering a thoughtfully selected range of furniture, decorative objects, and collectables spanning several centuries and styles.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality over quantity. Every piece on display has been personally chosen for its character, condition, and story, so you can shop with confidence knowing you are taking home something genuinely special.

Rooted firmly in the Edinburgh community, we are open at weekends and welcome both seasoned collectors and curious newcomers who simply love beautiful things with a history behind them.
