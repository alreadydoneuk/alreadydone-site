About Us

A passion for antiques, rooted in Edinburgh

6aHoweStreet is a family-run antique shop nestled in the heart of Edinburgh's Georgian New Town, bringing together a lovingly curated collection of antiques, vintage pieces, and original paintings from across the decades.

What sets us apart is the care and knowledge we bring to every item we stock. Each piece is personally selected for its character, quality, and story, ensuring that what you find here is genuinely special rather than mass-produced.

Located at 6A Howe Street, we have become a fixture of Edinburgh's independent shopping scene, and we are proud to offer free delivery on large items across the Edinburgh area, making it easy to bring a piece of history into your home.
