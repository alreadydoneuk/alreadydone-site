Who we are

A family passion for beautiful things

6aHoweStreet is a family-run antique and vintage shop situated at 6A Howe Street in Edinburgh's New Town, open throughout the week and at weekends for those who like to browse at their leisure. We bring together a carefully selected mix of antique furniture, vintage homewares and original paintings chosen for their character, quality and stories.

What sets us apart is a genuine eye for the unusual: pieces that feel both timeless and deeply liveable, sourced with the kind of care that only a family business truly invests. We offer a free delivery service for larger items throughout the Edinburgh area, so bringing something special home has never been easier.

Rooted in one of Edinburgh's most characterful streets, we are proud to be part of a neighbourhood that values craftsmanship, history and independent trade. Whether you are furnishing a home or searching for a one-off gift, we are always glad to help.
