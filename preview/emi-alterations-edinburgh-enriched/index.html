About

Crafted with care, every time

I am Emi, a seamstress based in Edinburgh with a lifelong passion for working with clothing and a genuine belief that the right fit transforms how you feel in what you wear.

My studio is at 134 Queensferry Road, and I am here throughout the week including at weekends, because I know that life does not always run to a Monday-to-Friday schedule.

Whether it is a cherished garment that needs careful repair, a suit that just needs to sit better, or a special occasion dress that has to be exactly right, I treat every project with the same level of attention and craft.
