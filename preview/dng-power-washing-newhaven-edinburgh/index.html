About Us

Edinburgh's Trusted Pressure Washing Specialists

DNG Power Washing is an Edinburgh-based pressure washing service built on hard work, attention to detail, and a commitment to leaving every surface spotless — whether it's a residential driveway in Niddrie or a commercial car park across the city.

What sets us apart is our use of professional-grade hot and cold water pressure washing equipment, combined with eco-friendly treatments that remove years of grime, moss, oil, and algae safely and effectively — without damaging your surfaces.

Based in Newhaven and serving clients across Edinburgh and the Lothians, we take pride in being a friendly, reliable local business that shows up on time, does the job right, and leaves your property looking brand new.

Our Services

What We Clean

From private driveways to large commercial areas, we handle pressure washing jobs of all sizes across Edinburgh and the Lothians.

🏠

Driveway & Patio Cleaning

Block paving, tarmac, concrete, or natural stone — we blast away years of dirt, oil stains, moss, and algae to restore your driveway or patio to its original condition.

🌿

Decking & Outdoor Areas

Wooden and composite decking can become dangerously slippery with algae build-up. Our soft wash and pressure treatments clean safely without splintering or warping.

🏢

Commercial Pressure Washing

Forecourts, car parks, retail units, and industrial estates — we provide reliable commercial cleaning that keeps your premises looking professional and well-maintained.

🧱

Render, Wall & Gutter Cleaning

Exterior walls, rendered surfaces, and clogged gutters can age a building fast. We remove unsightly green and black staining to restore kerb appeal quickly.
