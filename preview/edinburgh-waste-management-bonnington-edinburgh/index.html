About Us

Proudly local, genuinely reliable

Edinburgh Waste Management was born right here in Bonnington. Based at Bonnington Bond on Anderson Place, we've been serving households and businesses across Edinburgh for years — and the people who call us aren't just customers, they're our neighbours.

We started out with a simple belief: rubbish removal should be easy, honest, and fairly priced. No hidden charges, no last-minute let-downs. You call us, we turn up, we sort it. That's the deal.

Our team knows Edinburgh inside out — from Leith to Stockbridge, Portobello to Gorgie. We understand what local families and businesses need, and we take real pride in leaving every property cleaner than we found it. Recycling and responsible disposal are at the heart of everything we do, because this is our city too.
