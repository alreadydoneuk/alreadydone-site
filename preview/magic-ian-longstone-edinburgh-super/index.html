About Magic Ian

Magic Ian is a multi award-winning magician, children's entertainer and top-class balloon modeller based in Longstone, Edinburgh, with over 30 years of experience creating unforgettable moments at birthday parties, corporate events and community celebrations across the city and beyond.

What sets Ian apart is his rare ability to captivate every age in the room at once: from energetic games and dancing that get the youngest children moving, through to brilliantly comic magic and close-up illusions that leave parents and grandparents just as wide-eyed as the kids.

Ian is a long-serving member of the Edinburgh Magic Circle, having served as both President and Secretary for eight years, and is trusted by Edinburgh families weekend after weekend to deliver a show that is professional, punctual and genuinely magical.
