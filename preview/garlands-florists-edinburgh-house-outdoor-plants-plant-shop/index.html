About Us

Rooted in Pilton,
Blooming Across Edinburgh

We're Garlands Florists — a passionate, independent flower and plant shop nestled in the heart of Pilton, Edinburgh. Since opening our doors at 29 Elm Row, we've built our reputation one beautiful arrangement at a time, treating every customer's occasion as if it were our own.

We truly believe that flowers have the power to transform a moment — to say the things words sometimes can't. Whether you're planning the wedding of your dreams, celebrating a loved one, or simply want to fill your home with living colour, we pour genuine care into every stem we touch.

From our little corner of Edinburgh, we've had the privilege of being part of thousands of celebrations across the city and beyond. Come and visit us — we'd love to chat about what we can create for you.
