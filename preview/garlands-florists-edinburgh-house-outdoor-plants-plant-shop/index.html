What We Offer

Whether you're planning a wedding, styling a venue, or simply want to fill your home with beautiful plants, we have something for you.

💐

Wedding & Event Florals

Bespoke bridal bouquets, ceremony arches, table centrepieces, and buttonholes. We work closely with couples to design arrangements that reflect their vision — from classic white elegance to wild, botanical abundance. Full consultation included.

🌿

House & Office Plants

Transform your home or workplace with our curated selection of indoor plants. From trailing pothos to statement fiddle-leaf figs, we handpick healthy, thriving specimens and can advise on care, positioning, and styling for any space.

🪴

Outdoor & Garden Planting

Seasonal outdoor containers, patio planting schemes, and garden border plants sourced fresh and ready to thrive in Edinburgh's climate. We'll help you choose plants that will look stunning all year round — even through a Scottish winter.

🎁

Plant Accessories & Gifts

Our shop is stocked with beautiful pots, planters, tools, feeds, and plant-lover gifts. Whether you need a thoughtful birthday present or the perfect pot to complement your new plant, you'll find it at Garlands on Elm Row.

Rooted in Edinburgh, Passionate About Plants

Garlands Florists has been bringing colour, life, and natural beauty to homes and events across Edinburgh for years. Based in the heart of Pilton and with our shop at 29 Elm Row, we're a real local business — friendly faces who genuinely love what we do.

From the moment a customer walks through our door or reaches out about a wedding, we treat every order as something deeply personal. We believe flowers and plants aren't just decoration — they're how we mark the moments that matter most.

Whether you're nurturing your first houseplant or trusting us with the florals for the biggest day of your life, we bring the same warmth, expertise, and Edinburgh pride to everything we grow and create.
