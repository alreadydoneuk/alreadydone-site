About Us

Edinburgh's Specialist Stained Glass Studio

Edinburgh Stained Glass House is a dedicated stained glass studio based in Morningside, bringing together traditional glassworking techniques and a keen eye for contemporary design. What sets us apart is our commitment to hand-cutting every piece ourselves — no shortcuts, no outsourcing — so every commission reflects genuine artisan craftsmanship. We've spent years working with Edinburgh's homeowners, architects, and historic buildings, and we're proud to be a trusted part of this remarkable city's creative heritage.

What We Do

Our Services

Whether you need a repair, a restoration, or something created from scratch, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

🎨

Bespoke Commissions

We design and create one-of-a-kind stained glass pieces tailored precisely to your space, style, and brief — from decorative panels to full window installations for homes and commercial properties.

🏛️

Restoration & Repair

Specialist restoration of antique and period stained glass, including Victorian and Edwardian panels. We assess, conserve, and repair with sensitivity to the original design and historical character of the piece.

🪟

Architectural Glazing

Stained glass designed to integrate seamlessly into new builds and renovations — door panels, sidelights, skylights, and screens — working closely with architects and homeowners throughout Edinburgh.
