About Us

Framing Done Properly, Every Time

Stuart Martin Framing is an independent picture framing studio based on Inverleith Row in Bonnington, Edinburgh, offering a hands-on, expert service to customers who care about how their artwork and memories are presented.

What sets us apart is the attention to detail in every job we take on, from choosing the right moulding and mount board to ensuring the finished piece will stand the test of time. We never rush a frame, and we never cut corners.

As a local Edinburgh business, we know our customers personally and take pride in being a trusted name in the Bonnington community. Whether you walk in with a wedding photo or a treasured print, you will leave with something beautiful.

What We Offer

Our Framing Services

From classic fine art framing to bespoke commissions, we have the skills and materials to do justice to whatever you bring us.

🖼️

Bespoke Picture Framing

Custom frames made to measure for prints, paintings, photographs, and original artwork. We hold a wide selection of mouldings, glass types, and mount colours to suit any style or budget.

🏅

Memorabilia Framing

Jerseys, medals, programmes, certificates, and keepsakes all benefit from professional framing. We box-frame and mount items securely so they can be displayed with pride for years to come.

📷

Photography Framing

Wedding portraits, family photos, and professional photography deserve a frame that complements the image. We work with you to find the right combination of mount and moulding to make your photo shine.

🪞

Mirror Framing

Add a finishing touch to a plain mirror with a custom frame. We can frame mirrors to match your interior, whether you are after something minimal and modern or traditional and ornate.
