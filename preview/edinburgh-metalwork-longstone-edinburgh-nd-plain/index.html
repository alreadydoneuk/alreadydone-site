About Us

Skilled Metalwork, Right Here in Edinburgh

Edinburgh Metalwork is a professional blacksmithing workshop based in Longstone, bringing traditional craft skills and modern metalworking expertise to clients across the city and the Lothians.

What sets us apart is the combination of time-served skill and genuine attention to detail. Every piece we create is made to measure, built with quality materials, and finished to a standard that is designed to stand the test of time.

Rooted firmly in the local community, we are proud to serve homes and businesses throughout Edinburgh, and we are always happy to discuss your project in plain, straightforward terms.

Bespoke Gates and Railings
