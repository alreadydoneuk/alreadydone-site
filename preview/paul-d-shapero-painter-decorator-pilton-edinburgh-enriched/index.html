About us

Local Expertise. Consistent Quality.

Paul D Shapero is an experienced, Edinburgh-based painter and decorator serving Pilton, Broughton, Stockbridge, and the wider city. With years of hands-on experience across residential and commercial properties, Paul brings skill, care, and attention to every project, large or small.

Based just minutes from the city centre at Brougham Place, Paul is well placed to reach customers across North Edinburgh quickly and reliably. Weekend appointments are available to suit busy households, meaning you never have to take time off work to get the job done.

What sets Paul apart is his commitment to thorough preparation, honest communication, and leaving your home cleaner than he found it. From a single room refresh to a full property repaint, you can count on fair pricing and a result you will be proud of.
