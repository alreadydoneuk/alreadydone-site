About us

Distinguished Fabrication and Blacksmithing in Edinburgh

Edinburgh Metalwork is a distinguished fabrication and blacksmithing workshop based at our Longstone premises, dedicated to creating high-quality metalwork products that take your project from initial concept through to finished installation.

What sets us apart is our commitment to craftsmanship and precision: every piece we make is built to last, combining traditional blacksmithing techniques with modern fabrication methods to deliver results that genuinely impress.

Proudly rooted in Edinburgh, we serve homeowners, contractors, and businesses across the city and the wider Lothians, bringing skill, attention to detail, and a genuine passion for working with metal to every job we take on.

What we do

Our Metalwork Services

From decorative ironwork to structural steel fabrication, our Longstone workshop handles a wide range of metalwork projects for residential and commercial clients throughout Edinburgh.

Bespoke Gates and Railings

Hand-forged and fabricated gates, garden railings, balustrades, and security grilles designed and built precisely to your specifications and your property's style.

Structural Steel Fabrication

Supply and fabrication of structural steel elements for construction and renovation projects, including lintels, beams, and custom framing, delivered to site ready to install.

Decorative Ironwork

Bespoke decorative items including wall art, fire surrounds, furniture frames, and garden features, crafted using traditional blacksmithing techniques that create truly unique pieces.

Repairs and Restoration

Expert repair and restoration of existing metalwork, from historic wrought iron gates to modern steel structures, helping you preserve quality pieces and extend their working life.
