About Us

A distinguished workshop in the heart of Edinburgh

Edinburgh Metalwork is a professional fabrication and blacksmithing workshop based at Longstone, Edinburgh, dedicated to producing high-quality metalwork products for residential, commercial, and industrial clients across Scotland.

What sets us apart is our commitment to craftsmanship at every stage: from the initial design conversation through to the finished, fitted piece, we work closely with each client to ensure every detail meets their expectations.

Proudly rooted in the Edinburgh community, we bring traditional blacksmithing skills together with modern fabrication techniques to serve local homes, businesses, and contractors who value quality that lasts.

What We Do

Our Services

From bespoke gates and railings to structural steelwork, we handle a wide range of metalwork projects across Edinburgh and the Lothians.

🔨

Bespoke Blacksmithing

Handcrafted metalwork designed and forged to your specification. Whether decorative or functional, each piece is shaped with care and precision at our Longstone workshop.

🏠

Gates and Railings

Custom-made gates, garden railings, balustrades, and security barriers for residential and commercial properties. Built to last and tailored to complement your property.

⚙

Steel Fabrication

Structural and architectural steel fabrication for builders, contractors, and architects. We work to drawings or can advise on the best approach for your project.

🔧

Repairs and Restoration

Specialist repair and restoration of existing metalwork, including period ironwork, wrought iron features, and modern steel structures. Bring old ironwork back to life.
