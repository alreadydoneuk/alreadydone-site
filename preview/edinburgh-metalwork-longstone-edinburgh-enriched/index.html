About Us

Craftsmanship Forged in Edinburgh

Edinburgh Metalwork is a dedicated fabrication and blacksmithing workshop based at Longstone, crafting high-quality metalwork products from initial concept right through to finished installation. Our skilled team brings genuine artisan expertise to every project, whether you need a single bespoke piece or a full commercial specification run.

What sets us apart is our commitment to working closely with each client, combining traditional blacksmithing techniques with modern fabrication methods to achieve results that are both beautiful and built to last. Every item that leaves our workshop meets rigorous quality standards.

Rooted in the local community, we are proud to serve customers across Edinburgh, the Lothians, and beyond, bringing functional artistry to homes, businesses, and public spaces throughout the city we call home.

What We Do

Our Services

From ornamental ironwork to structural fabrication, we deliver precision craftsmanship tailored to your project requirements.

⛢

Bespoke Blacksmithing

Hand-forged ironwork crafted to your exact specification. Gates, railings, hinges, brackets, and decorative pieces made with traditional techniques and lasting quality.

⚙

Metal Fabrication

Precision cutting, welding, and forming of steel, aluminium, and other metals. Ideal for structural components, architectural elements, and industrial parts.

🏠

Gates and Railings

Custom-designed and fabricated gates, garden railings, balustrades, and security grilles for residential and commercial properties across Edinburgh.

✦

Bespoke Commissions

Unique sculptural pieces, furniture frames, fireplace surrounds, and one-of-a-kind metalwork. Bring your ideas and we will create something exceptional.
