About Penelope

Rooted in Edinburgh, in love with every petal

I'm Penelope, and flowers have been my language since I was a child helping my grandmother tend her garden in the Scottish Borders. After training in London and working across Europe, I came home to Edinburgh — the most beautiful city in the world — and opened Bloom Box in St James Quarter to bring that same joy to the people celebrating here.

For me, floristry isn't just about aesthetics. It's about understanding what a moment means to you — the colours that make your heart lift, the scent that feels like home — and translating all of that into something living and extraordinary.

Every event I take on is a privilege, and I approach each one with the same excitement and dedication, whether it's an intimate registry office ceremony or a grand celebration for five hundred guests.
