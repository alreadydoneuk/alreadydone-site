About us

Proudly building in Edinburgh for over a decade

Edinburgh Construction is a family-run building company based in Newhaven, Edinburgh, with more than ten years of hands-on experience delivering quality construction and renovation work across the city and surrounding communities.

We cover Newhaven, Leith, Stockbridge, Trinity, Granton, and the wider Edinburgh area — working on everything from modest home improvements to full residential extensions, with the same attention to detail regardless of project size.

What sets us apart is straightforward: we turn up when we say we will, we communicate clearly throughout every job, and we never cut corners. Our repeat customers and word-of-mouth reputation across North Edinburgh speak for themselves.
