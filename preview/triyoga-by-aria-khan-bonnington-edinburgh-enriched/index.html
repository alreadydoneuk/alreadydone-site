About Aria

A practice built around you, not a programme.

I am Aria Khan, a registered yoga instructor based here in Bonnington, Edinburgh, and I have been guiding people through the transformative power of movement and breath for over a decade. My training spans Hatha, Yin, and therapeutic yoga, and I hold a 500-hour RYT certification recognised by Yoga Alliance, alongside specialist study in trauma-sensitive practice and mindfulness-based stress reduction.

What matters most to me is the person on the mat, not just the posture. I began this work because I know firsthand how much a compassionate, skilled guide can change everything when life feels overwhelming. Yoga became my anchor, and now it is my honour to offer that same steadiness to others.

Every session I offer is shaped around your individual body, your nervous system, and your goals. I work with clients one-to-one and in small groups, both in person at my Edinburgh studio near London Road and online for those who prefer the comfort of home.
