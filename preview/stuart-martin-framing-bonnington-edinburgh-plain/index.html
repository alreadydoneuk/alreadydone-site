About Us

Trusted local framing in Edinburgh

Stuart Martin Framing is an independent picture framing studio based on Inverleith Row in Bonnington, Edinburgh, with years of experience helping customers give their artwork and photographs the presentation they deserve.

What sets us apart is our personal, hands-on approach to every job. Whether you bring in a family photograph, a limited-edition print, or a treasured piece of original artwork, we take the time to understand what matters to you and choose materials that complement it perfectly.

We are proud to serve the local community across Bonnington, Inverleith, Stockbridge, and the wider Edinburgh area, and we welcome every customer through our door with the same care and attention.

What We Offer

Our Framing Services

From bespoke frames to conservation-grade mounting, we offer a full range of professional framing solutions for every need and budget.

🖼

Bespoke Picture Framing

Custom-made frames cut and finished to your exact requirements. We carry an extensive range of mouldings, glass types, and mount colours so you can create a frame that truly fits your piece.

📷

Photo and Print Framing

Give your favourite photographs, family portraits, or limited-edition prints the professional presentation they deserve. We handle everything from single images to multi-aperture displays.

🎨

Artwork and Canvas Framing

We work with original paintings, watercolours, drawings, and canvas prints. Our framing enhances and protects your artwork while preserving its character and value for years to come.

🏅

Memorabilia and Keepsakes

Medals, certificates, sports memorabilia, jerseys, or sentimental objects. We create bespoke shadow box and deep box frames that display your keepsakes safely and stylishly.

🔬

Conservation Framing

For valuable or irreplaceable pieces, we offer conservation-grade framing using acid-free mounts, UV-protective glass, and archival materials to protect against fading and deterioration.

🗺

Map and Poster Framing

Large format maps, vintage posters, and oversized prints handled with care. We offer a range of frame and mount options to suit everything from decorative pieces to collectible items.
