About Us

Independently run hardware, built on local trust

Marchmont Hardware — trading as Hill Lord & Co — has been serving Edinburgh's residents, tradespeople, and DIY enthusiasts from our Warrender Park Road address for many years, building a reputation for having the right product and the right advice on hand.

What sets us apart is genuine expertise: our team knows their stock inside out, meaning you'll never walk away with the wrong screw gauge or an unsuitable adhesive. We stock a wide range of tools, fixings, paints, and household essentials that the big-box sheds often overlook.

We are proudly part of the Newhaven and Edinburgh community — we know our customers by name and we take real pride in keeping Edinburgh's homes, workshops, and small businesses moving.
