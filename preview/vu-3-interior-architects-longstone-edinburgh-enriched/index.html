About Us

A specialist studio with a national reach

VU-3 Interior Architects is a close-knit, specialist practice based in South Gyle, Edinburgh, focused entirely on the commercial sector. Our small, dedicated team brings precision and genuine creative thinking to every project, ensuring that each space we design serves its purpose beautifully and efficiently.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality over volume. We work with a select number of clients at any one time, which means every brief receives the focused attention and crafted detail it deserves, whether the project is a single office refurbishment or a multi-site rollout.

Though we call Longstone, Edinburgh home, our practice operates across the length and breadth of the United Kingdom, bringing the same standard of care and design rigour to every location we serve.

What We Do

Our Services

We offer a focused range of interior architecture services tailored to the commercial sector, delivered with care from initial concept through to completion.

Commercial Interior Design

Transforming offices, retail spaces, and hospitality environments into functional, inspiring interiors that reflect your brand and support your team.

Space Planning

Intelligent layout strategies that maximise every square metre, improving workflow, occupant wellbeing, and the overall efficiency of your commercial space.

Refurbishment Consultancy

Expert guidance through every stage of a commercial refurbishment, from feasibility and concept through to contractor coordination and final handover.

Concept and Specification

Detailed design concepts, material specifications, and finish schedules that give your project a clear visual direction and keep contractors aligned throughout.
