About Edinburgh Metalwork

Edinburgh Metalwork is a specialist blacksmithing business based in Longstone, Edinburgh, delivering high-quality bespoke metalwork for residential and commercial customers across the city and beyond. Our workshop at Unit 8, 9 Harvest Road is where traditional forge craft meets modern precision, producing work that stands the test of time.

What sets us apart is our hands-on approach: every piece is crafted individually to your specification, with no shortcuts and no compromise on quality. Whether you need a custom gate, decorative ironwork, or a structural metalwork solution, we bring genuine skill and attention to every job.

We are proud to be part of the Longstone community and have built a strong reputation across Edinburgh by treating every project as if it were our own home. Local customers trust us because we are reliable, straightforward, and passionate about the craft.

What We Do

Bespoke metalwork services crafted with precision and care, right here in Edinburgh.

🚪

Gates and Railings

Custom-forged gates and railings designed to complement your property. Built to last in Edinburgh's climate, with finishes that stay sharp for years.

🔩

Structural Metalwork

Beams, brackets, supports, and fixings fabricated and fitted to precise specification. Ideal for renovations, extensions, and commercial builds across the city.

✦

Decorative Ironwork

Bespoke decorative pieces including balusters, fire surrounds, candle holders, and wall art. Traditional blacksmithing technique, shaped to your vision.

🛠️

Repairs and Restoration

We repair and restore existing ironwork and metalwork, including period gates, railings, and heritage fittings. Sympathetic to the original craftsmanship.
