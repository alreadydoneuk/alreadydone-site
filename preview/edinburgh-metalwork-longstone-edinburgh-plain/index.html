About Us

Craft You Can Trust, Right Here in Edinburgh

Edinburgh Metalwork is a professional blacksmithing workshop based in Longstone, offering high-quality bespoke metalwork to homeowners, businesses, and tradespeople across the Edinburgh area.

What sets us apart is our commitment to genuine craftsmanship: every piece is designed and made in-house to your exact specification, combining traditional blacksmithing techniques with modern precision finishing.

Rooted in the local community of Longstone and the wider Edinburgh region, we take pride in building lasting relationships with our clients, delivering work that stands the test of time and reflects the character of the city.

What We Do

Our Metalwork Services

⚓

Gates and Railings

From driveway gates to garden railings, we design and fabricate bespoke ironwork that enhances the kerb appeal and security of your property.

⚿

Structural Steelwork

We supply and fit structural steel for residential and commercial projects, including lintels, beams, and frame supports, to builders and contractors across Edinburgh.

✎

Bespoke Ironwork

Unique decorative ironwork crafted to your brief, including balustrades, fire grates, furniture frames, signs, and one-off commissions for the home or garden.

🔧

Repairs and Restoration

We repair and restore existing metalwork, from period wrought iron to modern steel fittings, bringing worn or damaged pieces back to full working condition.
