About Edinburgh Metalwork

Your trusted local blacksmith, right here in Longstone.

Edinburgh Metalwork is a family-run blacksmith business based in Longstone, Edinburgh, with years of hands-on experience crafting and fabricating metalwork for homeowners, businesses, and contractors across the city and beyond.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality at every stage of the process. From the initial design consultation through to the finished piece, every project receives the same careful attention to detail and honest, straightforward advice. We work with iron, steel, and a range of other metals to deliver results that are both functional and genuinely well-made.

As a Longstone-based business, we are proud to serve our local Edinburgh community. Whether you need a bespoke gate for your home in Morningside, structural steelwork for a project in Leith, or decorative ironwork for a property in the New Town, we are close by and ready to help.

Our Services

From bespoke commissions to structural fabrication, we cover it all.

🔩

Bespoke Gates and Railings

Handcrafted gates, driveway entrances, and security railings designed and made to your exact specifications. Strong, stylish, and built to last Edinburgh's weather.

🛠

Structural Steelwork

Precision steel fabrication for construction and renovation projects. We work closely with builders and contractors across Edinburgh to deliver reliable structural metalwork on time.

✨

Decorative Ironwork

Traditional and contemporary decorative ironwork for homes, gardens, and heritage properties. Each piece is shaped and finished by hand in our Longstone workshop.

🔧

Repairs and Restoration

We repair and restore existing metalwork, including antique ironwork, damaged gates, and corroded railings. Bring old metalwork back to life with sympathetic, skilled craftsmanship.
