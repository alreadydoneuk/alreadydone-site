About Emi

Seamstress based in Edinburgh.

I take on the garments that matter to you, whether that is a wedding dress that needs to fit perfectly, a favourite jacket in need of rescue, or a tailored piece that deserves more than a generic off-the-peg result.

My approach is hands-on and straightforward: I listen carefully, I measure properly, and I do not cut until I am certain of the outcome. Precision is not optional in this craft, it is the point.

I work from Queensferry Road in Edinburgh and take bookings at weekends as well as through the week, so there is always a slot that fits your schedule.
