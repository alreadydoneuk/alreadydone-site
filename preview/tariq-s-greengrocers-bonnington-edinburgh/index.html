About Us

A Bonnington Institution Since the Early 2000s

Tariq's Greengrocers has been serving the Bonnington and Stockbridge communities from our spot on Deanhaugh Street for over two decades, built on a simple promise: the freshest produce, fairly priced, every single day.

What sets us apart is the care we put into every selection — Tariq personally checks stock each morning, sourcing seasonal Scottish produce alongside exotic fruits and specialist vegetables you simply won't find at the big supermarkets.

We're proud to be part of the fabric of this corner of Edinburgh, and our regulars — from young families to long-standing locals — keep coming back because they know they'll always be looked after.
