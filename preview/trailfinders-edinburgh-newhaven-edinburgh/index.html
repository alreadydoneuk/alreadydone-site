About Us

Passionate about travel, rooted in Edinburgh

Trailfinders Edinburgh is part of the UK's most trusted independent travel company, with a dedicated team based right here in the heart of Edinburgh at 14 Castle Street — just minutes from Newhaven and the wider city.

Unlike online booking platforms, every trip we arrange is put together by an experienced travel consultant who takes the time to understand exactly what you're looking for — matching your budget, your travel style, and your timescale with the right options, not just the easiest ones.

We're proud to serve Edinburgh's communities, from Newhaven and Leith to the New Town and beyond, and our loyal local clientele has trusted us to arrange everything from honeymoons in the Maldives to multi-stop round-the-world itineraries.
