About

Honest design from
Edinburgh's shoreline.

I'm a graphic and digital designer working independently from Newhaven, Edinburgh — I started Cake to do the kind of work I genuinely care about, for clients who want their brand to say something worth hearing.

My approach is straightforward: I listen properly before I put pencil to paper, I push for work that's considered rather than just clever, and I stay hands-on at every stage rather than delegating the interesting bits away.

Whether it's a brand built from scratch, a campaign that needs a sharp visual idea, or a digital product that has to actually work — I bring the same level of craft and directness to all of it.
