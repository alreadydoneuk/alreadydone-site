About Us

Edinburgh's Trusted Upholstery Specialists

Sofa & Chair Repairs is a renowned upholstery and furniture repair service based in Edinburgh, with over 35 years of hands-on experience restoring sofas, armchairs and cushions to their best.

Led by Russell, the team brings genuine skill and honesty to every job, offering free estimates, regular photo updates throughout the repair, and thorough follow-up checks after work is complete. No hidden costs, no shortcuts.

Cushion Restuffing
