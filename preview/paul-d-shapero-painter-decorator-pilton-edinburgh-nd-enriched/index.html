Edinburgh's Trusted Painter & Decorator

Paul has built a strong reputation across Edinburgh as a painter and decorator who combines genuine skill with a no-fuss, reliable approach. Based in Pilton and operating throughout the city, he brings years of hands-on experience to every project, large or small.
