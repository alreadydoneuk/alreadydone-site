About Ostara Pilates

A practice built on care, not performance

I'm a fully qualified Pilates instructor working right here in Pilton, Edinburgh, and I created Ostara Pilates because I believe movement should feel like something you look forward to — not another thing to get right. My training spans Classical and Contemporary Pilates methods, and I hold a Level 3 Diploma in Pilates Teaching alongside ongoing CPD in injury rehabilitation and mindful movement.

I came to Pilates myself during a period of chronic back pain and burnout, and what I found changed everything. It wasn't just about the physical strengthening — it was the quiet, the attention, and the sense of being in my own body again. That's the experience I want to offer every person who walks through my door.

Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing stress, navigating a major life change, or simply curious about what Pilates can offer you — you're welcome here. Sessions at Ostara are calm, personal, and always shaped around you as an individual.
