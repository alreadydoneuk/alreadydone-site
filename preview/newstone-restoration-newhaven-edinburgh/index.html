About Us

Trusted local restorers.

Newstone Restoration is a specialist flood damage recovery company based at Forth Street in Edinburgh, with a long track record of returning properties to their pre-loss condition — quickly, carefully, and with minimal disruption to you.

What sets us apart is our thorough, methodical approach: we don't just dry out surfaces — we assess hidden moisture, treat for mould risk, and restore structural integrity before any cosmetic work begins, so problems don't return months later.

We know Newhaven and the surrounding Edinburgh communities well, and we're proud to be the team local residents and businesses call when they need someone they can trust in a stressful situation.

What We Do

Our restoration services.

Water Extraction & Drying

Rapid removal of standing water using industrial-grade extraction equipment, followed by controlled drying using dehumidifiers and air movers to eliminate residual moisture from floors, walls and cavities.

Structural Drying & Assessment

Using thermal imaging and moisture mapping, we identify damage within walls, subfloors and joists that isn't visible to the naked eye — ensuring your property is genuinely dry before restoration work begins.

Mould Treatment & Prevention

Flood water creates ideal conditions for mould growth. We carry out professional antimicrobial treatment and apply protective coatings to affected surfaces, protecting your home and your family's health long term.

Reinstatement & Finishing

Once drying is complete, we manage the full reinstatement of affected areas — from replacing damaged plasterwork and flooring to redecorating — restoring your property to the condition it was in before the flood.
