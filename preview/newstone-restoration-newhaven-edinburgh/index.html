Who we are

What sets us apart is our hands-on approach: every job is assessed and managed by experienced restorers who combine industry-leading drying technology with genuine attention to detail, ensuring your property is fully dried, treated and returned to its pre-loss condition.

What we do

Our Restoration Services

💧

Emergency Water Extraction

Fast-response water removal using high-capacity pumps and extraction equipment. We act quickly to limit further damage to floors, walls and belongings from the moment we arrive on site.

🌬️

Structural Drying & Dehumidification

Professional-grade drying systems are deployed to remove embedded moisture from walls, subfloors and timbers — preventing secondary damage such as mould growth and structural weakening.

🧪

Mould Treatment & Prevention

Flood water creates the perfect conditions for mould. We treat affected surfaces with proven antimicrobial solutions and provide tailored advice to keep your property protected long after we've left.

🏠

Property Reinstatement

From stripped and treated substrates through to full redecoration, we manage the full restoration journey — coordinating with your insurer where needed so you don't have to handle everything alone.
