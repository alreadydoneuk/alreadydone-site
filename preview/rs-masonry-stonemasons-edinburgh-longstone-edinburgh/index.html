About RS Masonry

RS Masonry is an Edinburgh-based stonework specialist with years of hands-on experience restoring, repairing, and building with natural stone across the city and surrounding areas.

What sets us apart is our commitment to traditional craft methods combined with a thorough understanding of Edinburgh's sandstone and limestone buildings — we treat every job with the care it deserves, from pointing a garden wall to restoring a period façade.

Proudly serving Longstone and all Edinburgh neighbourhoods, we're local tradespeople who take genuine pride in the quality of work we leave behind.

Our Services

🪨

Stone Pointing & Repointing

We remove deteriorated mortar and replace it with a carefully matched mix that protects your masonry from Edinburgh's weather and extends its life for decades.

🏚️

Stone Restoration & Repair

Cracked, eroded, or spalling stonework carefully restored using traditional techniques — ideal for period tenements, boundary walls, and listed buildings.

🧱

Dry Stone Walling

Authentic dry stone walls built or repaired to last — perfect for gardens, driveways, and rural boundaries across Edinburgh and the Lothians.

🏗️

New Stone Build & Extensions

From stone garden features and steps to new builds and extensions, we work with architects and homeowners to achieve results that genuinely complement the local character.
