About Us

Edinburgh's Trusted Toastmaster

Waverley Toastmasters is a dedicated professional toastmaster service based in Edinburgh, proudly serving couples and event hosts at some of the city's most cherished venues, including the historic Augustine United Church on George IV Bridge.

What sets us apart is a genuine commitment to making every moment feel effortless, guiding your day with poise and good humour so you and your guests can be fully present and enjoy every occasion.

With deep roots in the Edinburgh community, we understand the character of local venues and the warmth that Scottish hospitality demands, giving your event an authentic and personal touch that no generic service can provide.

What We Offer

Our Services

Wedding Toastmaster

From the moment guests arrive to the final toast of the evening, your dedicated toastmaster ensures every announcement, introduction, and transition is handled with dignity and a warm smile. We coordinate closely with your venue, caterers, and wedding party so nothing is left to chance.

Civil Ceremonies

Whether you are planning an intimate civil ceremony or a larger formal celebration, Waverley Toastmasters brings the same level of professionalism and care to ensure proceedings flow smoothly, every cue is met, and your guests feel at ease throughout the occasion.

Corporate and Formal Events

Award dinners, gala evenings, charity fundraisers, and corporate celebrations all benefit from a confident, experienced toastmaster at the helm. We manage seating, announce speakers, and keep the programme on time, giving your event a polished and memorable finish.

Consultation and Planning Support

Not sure exactly what role a toastmaster plays or whether you need one? We offer straightforward consultations to talk through your event, explain how we work alongside your other suppliers, and help you plan a running order that keeps the day feeling natural and unforced. There is never any obligation, just honest, friendly advice from someone who knows Edinburgh's event scene inside out.
