About Us

Interior architecture rooted in Edinburgh

VU-3 Interior Architects is an Edinburgh-based practice specialising in intelligent interior design and architecture for residential, commercial, and hospitality projects. We combine creative vision with rigorous technical knowledge to deliver spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

What sets us apart is our commitment to genuinely collaborative working. Every project begins with listening: understanding how you use your space, what you want it to feel like, and what it needs to achieve. That clarity shapes every decision we make from first sketch through to final installation.

Based at South Gyle and serving clients across Longstone, Corstorphine, Murrayfield and the wider Edinburgh area, we bring a local understanding of the city's distinct architecture and planning context to every project we undertake.
