What we do

Services we offer

From blocked gutters to sparkling windows, we take care of the jobs that keep your home looking its best and protected from Edinburgh's weather.

🏠

Gutter Cleaning

We clear out leaves, debris, and blockages from your gutters so rainwater flows freely and away from your foundations. Ideal before the Scottish autumn sets in.

💧

Gutter Inspection

Not sure if your gutters are coping? We carry out a thorough check for cracks, sagging sections, or loose fixings, and give you an honest report on what we find.

🪟

Window Cleaning

Professional exterior window cleaning using streak-free methods. We leave your windows clear and bright, giving your home a fresh, well-kept appearance all year round.

🧹

Fascia and Soffit Cleaning

We clean your fascias and soffits to remove the grime and green algae that builds up over time. It protects the wood and helps your home stay looking sharp.

About us

A friendly local service you can rely on

I'm Stephen O'Rourke, and I run S O'Rourke Window Cleaning Services from my home here in Edinburgh, just off Craigmount Bank in EH4. This isn't a big franchise operation. It's a one-person business built on doing a proper job and being easy to deal with.

Edinburgh's weather can be hard on homes. Gutters fill up quickly, windows pick up grime from the city air, and it's easy for small maintenance tasks to slide. That's where I come in. I work weekends so that busy families and working homeowners don't have to take time off just to get essential jobs done.

I take real pride in the work I do, and I treat every home as if it were my own. Whether you've got a tenement flat in the city or a semi-detached out towards Corstorphine, I'll turn up on time, do the job properly, and leave everything tidy behind me.
