Who we are

Craft, Quality and Character

Edinburgh Metalwork is a distinguished blacksmithing workshop based in Longstone, Edinburgh, dedicated to creating bespoke, high-quality metalwork that is guided by your vision and shaped by our skill. We are a registered Private Limited Company (SC729933), built on a foundation of craft, integrity, and genuine attention to detail.

What sets us apart is our complete concept-to-completion approach. We work closely with every client from the very first sketch through to the final installation, ensuring the finished piece reflects exactly what was imagined. Our fabrication standards are uncompromising, and our blacksmithing tradition is carried forward with pride in every project we take on.

Proudly rooted in Edinburgh, we serve clients across the city and the wider surrounding area. Whether your project is residential, commercial, or architectural, our workshop is ready to bring your metalwork ideas to life with the care and precision they deserve.
