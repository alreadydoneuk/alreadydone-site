What we offer

Floristry for every occasion

Whether you're planning the wedding of your dreams or a last-minute celebration, we offer a full range of floral services tailored to your event and budget.

💐

Wedding Floristry

Full wedding packages covering bridal bouquets, bridesmaid posies, buttonholes, ceremony arch, pew ends, reception centrepieces and evening florals. We handle everything from your initial consultation to the final petal on the day.

🎉

Events & Celebrations

Birthdays, engagements, baby showers, anniversaries and corporate events. We design eye-catching statement pieces, table arrangements and entrance displays that set the tone from the moment guests arrive.

🏢

Corporate Floristry

Regular office flowers, product launches, award ceremonies and conference styling. We work with Edinburgh businesses of all sizes to bring natural beauty into professional spaces, with flexible weekly or event-by-event arrangements.

🌿

Bespoke & Sympathy

Seasonal hand-tied bouquets, luxury gift arrangements, and compassionate funeral floristry. Each piece is designed with care, using the freshest Scottish and seasonal stems available, delivered across Edinburgh and the Lothians.

Our story
🌸 Based in Pilton, Edinburgh

Passion rooted right here in Edinburgh

I'm Karen McDowell, and flowers have been my world for over fifteen years. Working from our studio on Gorgie Road, I've had the privilege of creating arrangements for hundreds of Edinburgh families, couples, and businesses — each one a reminder of why I fell in love with this craft in the first place.

Growing up in Pilton, I know this city intimately — its character, its weather, and the warmth of the people who live here. That local knowledge means I can source the freshest seasonal stems, recommend the right flowers for Edinburgh's unpredictable climate, and deliver on time, every time.

Every event I work on feels personal to me. When you trust me with your flowers, I don't just want them to look beautiful — I want them to feel exactly right for you and your story.
