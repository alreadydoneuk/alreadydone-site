About Us

Skilled Craftspeople, Right Here in Edinburgh

Ebony Hill Furniture Repair is a dedicated furniture restoration workshop based at our unit in Newbattle, Dalkeith, proudly serving customers across Pilton, Edinburgh and the surrounding area. Whether you have a treasured antique, a family heirloom, or a modern piece that has seen better days, our experienced team brings expert craftsmanship to every job.

What sets us apart is our genuine care for the pieces we restore. We treat every item as if it were our own, using quality materials and time-honoured techniques to achieve results that last. We have built a strong reputation locally through honest advice, fair pricing, and work that speaks for itself.

Being part of the Edinburgh community matters to us. We know how much a well-loved piece of furniture can mean to a home, and we take pride in helping local families keep those pieces for generations to come.

What We Do

Our Services

From a single scratch to a full structural overhaul, we handle all aspects of furniture repair and restoration with skill and care.

🪑

Furniture Restoration

We restore antique and vintage furniture to its former glory, preserving original character while making it structurally sound and beautiful once more. Suitable for chairs, tables, cabinets and more.

🔨

Structural Repairs

Wobbly joints, broken legs, split frames or damaged drawer runners, we diagnose and repair structural faults so your furniture is safe, stable, and built to last for years to come.

🎨

Surface Refinishing

Scratches, water stains, fading and worn finishes are no match for our refinishing service. We strip, sand, and reapply professional-grade finishes to give your furniture a refreshed, polished appearance.

🛋️

Upholstery Repair

Torn fabric, sagging cushions, or worn seat pads? We offer upholstery repair and re-covering to bring softness and style back to your sofas, armchairs, dining chairs and more.
