Who We Are

Edinburgh's Trusted Scaffolding Specialists

Rise Access & Scaffolding Solutions is a locally rooted scaffolding company based at Royal Elizabeth Yard, Kirkliston, serving Longstone, Edinburgh, and communities right across the Lothians and beyond. We've built our reputation one scaffold at a time — with a hands-on, professional approach that keeps clients coming back.

With over a decade of experience in the trade, our team has worked on everything from single-storey extensions in Corstorphine to multi-storey commercial developments in the city centre. We know Edinburgh's buildings, its weather, and the particular demands that come with working in one of Scotland's most architecturally varied cities.

What sets us apart is straightforward: we show up on time, we communicate clearly, we work safely, and we price fairly. No hidden costs, no surprises — just solid, dependable scaffolding you can trust.
