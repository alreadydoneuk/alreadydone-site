Our Story

Passionate about dogs, proud to be Edinburgh's own

Super Dog Training Academy was founded right here in Edinburgh with one simple belief: every dog deserves to be understood, not just managed. We're a small, dedicated team of dog lovers based on Brunswick Road in the heart of the city — and we genuinely care about every animal in our charge.

We've been part of the Edinburgh community for years, building relationships with local families across Leith, Hillside, Abbeyhill, and beyond. Many of our clients have been with us since their dogs were puppies — and that loyalty means the world to us.

We keep group sizes intentionally small so your dog always receives real attention. No rushed routines, no conveyor-belt care. Just a warm, familiar environment that your dog will look forward to visiting.
