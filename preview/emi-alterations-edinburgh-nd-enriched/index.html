About Emi

A seamstress who takes the work seriously

I'm Emi, and I run my alterations studio from Edinburgh, working with clients across the city who care about how their clothes fit and last. My background is in precision tailoring, and I bring that same discipline to every job, whether it's a small repair or a full bespoke commission.

I offer a multilingual service, so language is never a barrier, and I work around you, including at weekends, because fitting appointments should be convenient. I believe the best alterations are the ones nobody notices: the garment simply looks as if it was always that way.
