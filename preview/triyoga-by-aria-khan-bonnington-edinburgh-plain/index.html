About Aria

A practice built on genuine connection

I am Aria Khan, a certified yoga instructor based right here in Bonnington, Edinburgh. I came to yoga not as a fitness pursuit, but as a way through a difficult chapter in my own life, and that personal journey is at the heart of everything I offer today. I hold a 500-hour Yoga Alliance accreditation, with specialist training in restorative yoga, breathwork, and trauma-informed practice.

After years of teaching in studios across Scotland, I made the decision to work with people in a more intentional, one-to-one and small-group setting. I wanted to slow down, listen more, and really understand the person in front of me before anything else. My sessions draw on Hatha, Yin, and mindfulness traditions, woven together in a way that feels natural and sustainable for your life.

Whether you have never unrolled a yoga mat before or you are returning after years away, you are very welcome here. This is a space without judgement, where your pace is always the right pace.

How It Works

Simple, thoughtful, and at your pace

01

Free Initial Consultation

We begin with a relaxed conversation, in person in Bonnington or via video call. There is no commitment, no pressure, and no yoga required. I simply want to hear about you, what you are carrying, and what you are hoping for.

02

Your Personalised Practice

Based on what we discussed, I design a practice that fits your body, your schedule, and your goals. Sessions can be one-to-one, in small groups, or a blend of both, with home practice guidance included.

03

Ongoing Support

As you settle into your practice, we check in regularly and adapt as life changes. You are never handed a fixed programme and left to get on with it. I am here for the whole journey, however long that takes.
