About Us

Built on Skill, Honesty, and Local Know-How

ARJ Builders Edinburgh is a family-run building company based at Russell Road Industrial Estate in Bonnington, and we've been delivering high-quality extensions and structural work across Edinburgh and the Lothians for over 15 years.

We cover Bonnington, Leith, Stockbridge, Morningside, Portobello, and all surrounding Edinburgh neighbourhoods — our deep familiarity with local planning requirements and Edinburgh's traditional tenement and sandstone properties means fewer surprises and smoother projects for you.

What sets us apart is our hands-on approach: our experienced tradespeople manage every stage from initial design and building warrant applications through to the final clean finish, so you always deal with someone who genuinely cares about the quality of your home.
