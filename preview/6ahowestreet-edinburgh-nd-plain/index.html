About Us

A considered collection at the heart of Edinburgh

6aHoweStreet is a carefully curated antique shop nestled in one of Edinburgh's most characterful Georgian streets, welcoming collectors, decorators, and curious browsers every weekend.

What sets us apart is our eye for quality over quantity: every piece in the shop has been selected for its character, condition, and story, so you will never wade through clutter to find something truly special.

Rooted in the New Town's rich architectural heritage, we are proud to be part of a neighbourhood that has always valued craftsmanship and lasting beauty.
