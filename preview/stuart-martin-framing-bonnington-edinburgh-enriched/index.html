About Us

A Framer You Can Trust

Stuart Martin Framing is an independent picture framing studio based on Inverleith Row in Bonnington, Edinburgh, with years of hands-on experience helping customers present their artwork, prints, photographs, and memorabilia beautifully.

What sets us apart is a genuine commitment to quality: every frame is chosen and fitted with care, using materials that protect your pieces for the long term. There are no shortcuts here, and that shows in the finished result.

We are proud to be part of the local Edinburgh community. Whether you have a treasured family photograph, a limited-edition print, or a piece of original art, we give every item the individual attention it deserves.

What We Offer

Picture Framing Services

From a single photo to a gallery wall, we offer a full range of framing services tailored to your needs and budget.

🖼️

Bespoke Picture Framing

Custom-made frames sized and finished to suit your exact artwork. We work with a wide selection of mouldings, mounts, and glazing options to create something truly unique.

📸

Photo and Print Framing

Preserve your favourite photographs and limited-edition prints in frames that complement the image. Archival materials are available to protect against fading and damage.

🎨

Original Artwork Framing

We handle original paintings, drawings, and mixed-media pieces with the extra care they require. Conservation-quality mounting and glazing options are available on request.

🏆

Memorabilia and Keepsakes

Sports shirts, certificates, maps, fabric pieces, and other items of sentimental value can all be beautifully framed as display pieces or lasting gifts for someone special.
