About Us

Skin Care That Goes Deeper Than the Surface

Skin N Tonic Aesthetics is a professional aesthetics clinic based in Bonnington, Edinburgh, offering a carefully curated range of non-surgical treatments carried out by experienced and fully qualified practitioners who put your safety and comfort first.

What sets us apart is our commitment to natural-looking, personalised results — we take the time to understand your skin goals before recommending any treatment, so you leave feeling like the best version of yourself, never overdone.

Rooted in the local Edinburgh community, we're proud to serve clients from across the city and beyond, building lasting relationships built on trust, transparency, and consistently outstanding outcomes.

Our Treatments

What We Offer

From anti-ageing injectables to advanced skin rejuvenation, our treatments are tailored to your unique skin — always safe, always professional.

💉

Anti-Wrinkle Injections

Smooth fine lines and prevent new ones forming with precision-placed botulinum toxin treatments. Subtle, effective, and expertly administered for a naturally refreshed look.

✨

Dermal Fillers

Restore volume and contour to lips, cheeks, and jawline using premium hyaluronic acid fillers. Results are immediate and tailored entirely to your facial anatomy.

🌿

Skin Boosters & Hydration

Deeply nourish and rehydrate your skin from within using injectable moisturisers that improve texture, elasticity, and glow — ideal for dull or dehydrated complexions.

🔬

Advanced Skin Treatments

Target pigmentation, acne scarring, and uneven skin tone with our advanced aesthetic treatments including chemical peels and microneedling, customised for your skin type.
