About

Hi, I'm Dean.

I started Already Done because I kept noticing the same thing — brilliant local businesses with no website, or one that was broken, or one that hadn't been touched since 2011. Customers were finding them on Google and hitting a dead end.

So I built a system that finds those businesses and builds them a site before we've even spoken. You get to see what you're buying before you spend a penny. If it's not right, tell me and I'll fix it. If you don't want it, no problem.

There's no agency, no team, no hard sell. Just me, making websites for local businesses that actually need one.
